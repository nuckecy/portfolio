### Design Principles

#### üéØ Show, Don't Overwhelm
Complexity should be revealed gradually, not dumped on users all at once. **Start simple.** Demonstrate competence step by step. Users gain confidence through progressive discovery - not by seeing everything upfront.

#### üåç Include, Don't Retrofit
Accessibility and cultural diversity shape the foundation - they don't get added later. **Design for different thinking styles, communication preferences, and interaction needs from day one.** Compliance is the baseline. True inclusion is the goal.

#### üîß Fix Systems, Not Symptoms
Surface problems usually point to deeper issues. **Address root causes** through interconnected solutions rather than patching individual pain points. Sustainable design solves for the system, not just the screen.

#### ‚úì Validate, Then Build
Every significant design decision should be backed by evidence - user insights, behavioral data, or tested assumptions. **Intuition starts the conversation, but research validates the direction.** Make design choices defensible.

## Experience

### Zalando SE | Berlin, Germany
**Senior Product Designer, Customer Care Pillar** | Nov 2021 - Present

I lead the end-to-end design for the customer care division at Europe's largest fashion e-commerce platform. My focus has been on initiatives that improve customer support experiences while making our operations more efficient.

#### Project: Customer Care - Contextual Self-Help

**Problem:** We were dealing with approximately 20 million customer inquiries every year. It was a huge problem, and we needed a better way to solve customer challenges without them having to make multiple contacts.

**Solution:** I designed a data-driven contextual support system that gave customers personalized self-service options, often solving their problem in just one go. My technical background as a UX engineer allowed me to collaborate with engineering on a smart content delivery API that cut down page load time by 35%.

**Impact:**
- Reduced support ticket volume by 42%
- Decreased resolution time from over 24 hours to under 3 hours
- Improved customer satisfaction scores by 28%
- Generated ‚Ç¨3.4M in annual operational savings

#### Project: Guided Conversational Chatbot

**Problem:** Our old chatbot was pretty rigid, which meant a 78% abandonment rate and a 43% escalation to human agents. Customers felt trapped in loops and kept having to repeat themselves, which was not a good look for the brand and was damaging our reputation.

**Solution:** By analyzing over 100 customer histories and use cases, I discovered useful insights and designed a new conversational architecture. This new system used natural language understanding with high accuracy and dynamic conversation paths that could adapt to a customer's emotional cues. We also included multi-modal confirmation states (visual, textual, and progress indicators) to keep customers confident throughout the interaction.

**Impact:**
- Increased the automation rate by 34%, beating our target by 9%
- Reduced the average handling time by 47%
- Improved the first-contact resolution rate by 23%

#### Project: Accessibility Transformation Initiative

**Problem:** After a comprehensive accessibility audit, we discovered that 78% of our customer-facing interfaces and touchpoints had major WCAG compliance issues. This was a big deal because it meant we were effectively excluding about 15% of our potential customers and facing significant legal and reputational risks in our key European markets.

**Solution:** As a key part of a company-wide initiative, I drove the WCAG compliance solution for the Customer Care touchpoints. I optimized and tested all the designs to achieve AA and AAA accessibility compliance for web forms, FAQs, and contextual self-help tools to meet the full European Accessibility Act compliance by June 2025, which included implementing dark mode and dynamic text capabilities.

**Impact:**
- Achieved WCAG 2.1 AA compliance across 94% of customer interfaces
- Expanded potential market by approximately 2.3 million new customers
- Reduced legal compliance risk by 65%
