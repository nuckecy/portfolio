## Design Philosophy

I believe great design emerges from deep understanding of user needs combined with business objectives. My approach emphasizes:

1. **Context over comprehensiveness** - Delivering the right information at the right moment
2. **Progressive disclosure** - Revealing complexity only when needed
3. **System thinking** - Designing for scalability and consistency
